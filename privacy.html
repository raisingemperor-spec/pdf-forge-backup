PDF Forge Privacy Policy

Welcome to PDF Forge. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it through our compliance with this policy.

1. Data Handling & Security

**We Do Not Store Your Files.** All PDF merging, splitting, compressing, and conversion processes are performed entirely within your web browser (client-side). Your files are never uploaded to our servers or stored on our infrastructure. Once you close the browser, your data is gone.

2. Data Collected by Third Parties (Google AdSense)

We use Google AdSense to serve advertisements on our website. This service uses cookies to serve ads based on a user’s prior visits to this and other websites.

  • **Cookies:** AdSense uses cookies to track user behavior to serve relevant, personalized ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ad Settings.
  • **Non-Personal Data:** We collect non-personal data (such as browser type, operating system, and pages visited) using Google Analytics to understand traffic flow and improve the site.

3. Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

This policy is effective as of November 2025.
